## 2024-05-23 - [Documentation UX]
**Learning:** In repositories consisting solely of documentation and configuration (no app code), "UX" translates to "Reader Experience" (RX) or "Contributor Experience" (CX). Simple navigation aids like "Back to Top" links are valid Palette improvements.
**Action:** When working on docs-only repos, look for opportunities to improve navigation, readability, and information architecture rather than UI components.
