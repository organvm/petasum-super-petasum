## 2024-05-23 - Logic-First Governance UX
**Learning:** This repository is a "Logic-First" governance template, not a standard app. Users are expected to follow a strict logical framework (Premises -> Logic -> Conclusion). The existing templates were too generic and didn't prompt for this specific structure, likely leading to low-quality submissions or frustration.
**Action:** Align issue templates (specifically RFCs) with the `LOGIC_FRAMEWORK.md` structure to guide users into the "pit of success" by making the right way the easy way.
