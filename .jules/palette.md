## 2024-05-23 - Enforcing Logic-First via Issue Templates
**Learning:** UX in governance repos isn't just about UI labels; it's about explicitly guiding the user's thought process. Adding a specific "Logical Analysis" field with structured placeholders (Premise/Conclusion) reduces the cognitive load of adhering to abstract frameworks like "Logic-First".
**Action:** When designing templates for strict governance models, bake the requirements into the form fields rather than relying solely on external documentation links.
